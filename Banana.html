BANANA VARIETIES

Amruthapani

Amruthapani variety are very special variety in banana as this fruit have very good flavour and fragrance which makes this variety differ from other variety.

Besrai

Besrai is a leading commercial variety of Maharashtra. The plant stature is Dwarf making it less prone to wind damage.

Elakki Bale

This variety is known as Elaichi Banana in Mumbai, Yelakki in Bengaluru and Chinia in Bihar.

Blue Java

Blue Java have vanilla ice-cream like flavor, and are also famous as ‘Ice cream bananas.’

Nanjangud

Nanjangud variety is the most important crop in India. It is grown in Mysore and Chamarajanagar district of Karnataka, India.

Praying Hands

Praying Hands bananas are an unusual variety with two adjoining ‘hands’ that grow merged, hence the name.
